Reactive Forms

Reactive forms in Angular provide a way to create and manage forms using a reactive programming approach. They allow for more complex form validation, dynamic form controls, and better separation of concerns. Reactive forms are built using the FormGroup, FormControl, and FormArray classes. Reactive forms are more scalable and maintainable, especially for larger applications.
